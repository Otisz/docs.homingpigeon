# Country ENUM

## Used in
- [Personal information](/schema-viza/segments/personal-information#nationality)

## Values
| English        | Hungarian    |
|:---------------|:-------------|
| `^[a-z]{2}$/i` | `^[A-Z]{2}$` |

_I'm not going to copy-paste every country here... regex is representing them._
