# Visa schema

## Used in
- [Checkin guest](/schemas/viza/segments/checkin-guest#visa)

## Properties
### `authorizationNumber`
The visa or residence permit number of a third-country visitor.
- Type: `string` | `null`

### `entryDate`
The date of entry into Hungary for a visitor from a third country.
- Type: `string` | `null`
- Rules: `format: 'Y-m-d'`

### `entryPoint`
The place of entry into Hungary for a visitor from a third country.
- Type: `string` | `null`
